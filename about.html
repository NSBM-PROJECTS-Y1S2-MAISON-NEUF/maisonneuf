About Us

Maison Neuf, the House of Nine, was born out of the passion and creativity of nine fashion enthusiasts at NSBM, Sri Lanka. We came together, driven by a shared dream to bring elegance, sustainability, and innovation to the fashion industry. Each of us brings a unique perspective, blending traditional Sri Lankan aesthetics with contemporary design.

Our journey began in the bustling classrooms of NSBM, where we spent countless hours brainstorming, sketching, and discussing how we could leave a mark on the fashion world.
